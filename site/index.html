Welcome to solo D&D adventuring!

This is the output of an automatically generated adventure for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition. We assume that you will be playing with a single Player Character, whose Class may be whatever you feel like playing!

Adding a sidekick is possible, although we would recommend following the Sidekick rules from the Essentials Kit, rather than those presented on Tasha's Cauldron of Everything – for sidekicks must become more useless as the adventure unfolds and your character grows.

Monster presented on this adventure – which will be shown in bold letters for ease of spotting – are those available under the System Reference Document. We recommend this site as an intuitive source.

And last, this adventure assumes fairness in play. E.g., when we ask for a skill check, we will display its Difficulty Class (DC). Try and play as if you did not know this kind of information!
